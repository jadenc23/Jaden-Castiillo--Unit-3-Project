Survive The Night

This story begins in your residence at Mandela County. The News Broadcast has advised residents to stay home and to be armed. Reports of creatures who disguise as you and try to invade your home roam the town. It is around midnight, you've just put the 8-year-old that you were babysitting to sleep and go downstairs to relax in the living room. You see a figure standing outside your window.

The figure seems to be more clear the closer you get to your window. It looks familar even...it looks like...you??!

The figure disappears from your window leaving you confused. All of a sudden the lights go out leaving you in complete darkness.

You back away from the window in utter fear as the figure stares you down. You realize it's one of those creatures that the broadcast was warning you about. The creature breaks through the window as it slowly creeps in your house. You spare no hesitation and run straigh to your garage to grab your firearm. You close the door behind you and grab your shotgun. The door opens and you fire out of pure instinct. You stare down at the body on the floor and your heart drops to your stomach. It wasn't the creature you just put to disease. It was the kid you were babysitting that came down to see what all the noise was. You drop down crying, the creature pops out staring while smiling menacingly.

YOU DID NOT SURVIVE THE NIGHT.

You panic and pull out your phone's flashlight. It's silent. You decide to act and run up the stairs to wake up the kid. You burst into the room and pure horror induces in your body as you see the creature digesting the poor child.
